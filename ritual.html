Processing methods

How the fruit changes the cup.

Washed

Fruit is removed before drying. Usually clean, bright and transparent.

Honey

Some mucilage remains during drying. Often sweet, rounded and balanced.

Natural / dry

Whole cherries dry around the seed. Often fruity, full and expressive.

Wet hulled

A regional method associated with earthy depth, body and lower acidity.
